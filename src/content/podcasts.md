# Podcasts

Firmium lets you subscribe to podcasts by RSS feed and play episodes alongside your music library.

## Subscribing

Go to the **Podcasts** section and tap **Add podcast**. Paste the podcast's RSS feed URL — most podcast websites list this under a "RSS" or "subscribe" link. Firmium fetches the feed and adds it to your subscriptions.

## Browsing and playing

Tap a podcast to see its episode list, newest first. Tap **Play** on any episode to start listening through the same player used for your music.

If you pause an episode partway through, Firmium remembers where you left off — tap **Play** again later and it picks up from that point.

## Refreshing

New episodes don't appear automatically yet. Open a podcast and tap **Refresh** to check for new episodes.

## Unsubscribing

Open a podcast and tap **Unsubscribe** to remove it and its episodes.

## Notes

- Subscriptions are stored locally on each device — they don't sync between your desktop and Android installs.
- Downloading episodes for offline listening isn't supported yet.
