# Overview

Firmium is a music player for your own music library. It connects to a [Navidrome](https://www.navidrome.org/) server (or any other server that speaks the OpenSubsonic API) and gives you a clean, fast interface for browsing and playing your music on Linux desktop and Android.

## What Firmium does

- Browses your albums, artists, songs, and playlists
- Plays music with smooth crossfade or gapless transitions between tracks
- Shows lyrics (synced or plain) and artist biographies
- Lets you customize the look with built-in or custom color themes
- Remembers your login so you don't have to sign in every time
