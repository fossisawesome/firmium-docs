# Discovery & Smart Radio

Firmium can keep the music going and help you find more to listen to, on both desktop and Android.

## Continue playing after the queue ends

Turn on **Settings → Playback → Continue playing after queue ends** and Firmium will keep playing once your queue runs out. When the last track finishes, it adds about ten more songs similar to what you were just listening to, and keeps doing so for as long as you listen. It never interrupts a playing queue — it only steps in once the queue is empty, and it avoids repeating songs you've already heard this session.

The suggestions come from your server's similar-tracks feature when available, and otherwise from your own library, matched by genre and tempo (BPM) to the last track.

This is off by default.

## Mix

Open **Mix** from the sidebar (desktop) or the bottom navigation (Android) to build a playlist around a mood:

1. Pick an energy level — **Chill** (slower songs), **Mid** (mid-tempo), or **High** (faster songs).
2. Optionally narrow it to a single genre.
3. Start the mix. Firmium gathers matching songs from your library, shuffles them, and starts playing.

## Start Radio

Want a station built around one thing? Use **Start Radio**:

- **Desktop** — open the "…" menu on any track, album, or artist and choose **Start Radio**.
- **Android** — long-press a track or album (or use the Start Radio action on an artist page).

Firmium seeds a fresh queue from that item and starts playing immediately, using the same similar-tracks-then-genre/tempo matching as Continue playing.

## You might also like

Artist pages show a **You might also like** section with related artists. To keep every suggestion playable, Firmium only lists artists you already have music from in your library. Tap one to jump to that artist.
